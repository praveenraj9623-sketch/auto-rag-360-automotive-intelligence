Title: Phase 1 Sample Supplier Notice
Document type: supplier_notice
Sensitivity: supplier_visible

Suppliers may receive component-level quality notices when a field issue may
involve supplied parts. The notice should include part number, component family,
inspection criteria, quarantine expectations, and a due date for containment
feedback.

For brake hose brackets, suppliers should confirm fixture settings, material lot
traceability, and corrective action status without accessing internal service
manuals or confidential engineering analyses.
