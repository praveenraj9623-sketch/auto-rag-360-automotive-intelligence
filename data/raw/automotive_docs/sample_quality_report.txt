Title: Phase 1 Sample Quality Report
Document type: quality_report
Sensitivity: internal_engineering

The quality engineering review found that brake hose routing variation on
selected crossover vehicles increased abrasion risk near the front wheel well.
The containment action is an inspection gate at final assembly and a supplier
process capability review for the affected hose bracket.

Root cause analysis for intermittent instrument cluster resets points to a
software watchdog timing issue during cold start. The corrective action is a
calibration update and expanded cold chamber validation.
