Document type: service_manual
Sensitivity: internal_engineering

Brake actuator diagnostic procedure:
When the service brake warning lamp is paired with diagnostic code BA-142,
inspect the hydraulic control unit connector, verify brake pressure sensor
calibration, and confirm actuator torque values after replacement. This
procedure is intended for trained service engineers and internal engineering
teams.

Air bag control module connector check:
For intermittent restraint module communication, inspect the under-seat wiring
harness and confirm the connector latch is fully seated before clearing fault
codes. Do not replace the module until the wiring continuity test passes.
