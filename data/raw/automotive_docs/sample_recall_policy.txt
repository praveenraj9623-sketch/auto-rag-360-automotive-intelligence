Title: Phase 1 Sample Recall Policy
Document type: recall_policy
Sensitivity: confidential

Recall triage begins when field reports, warranty claims, consumer complaints,
supplier notices, or engineering test failures indicate a safety-related defect
trend. The review board compares severity, occurrence rate, detectability, and
available remedies before deciding whether to open a formal recall action.

Executives and quality leaders should receive a summary of affected population,
potential consequence, proposed owner notification timing, and remedy readiness.
